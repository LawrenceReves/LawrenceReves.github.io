Myceliyum is a conceptual psilocybin mushroom organization that legally produces and distributes a range of "magic mushrooms" products in Canada. Through thorough research and consideration for sensitivity of the subject matter — a respectable yet exuberant line of products, and website were designed — presenting medicinal, as well as recreational use edibles alongside interactive, and informational platforms to properly educate individuals on the pros and cons of psilocybin mushrooms.
